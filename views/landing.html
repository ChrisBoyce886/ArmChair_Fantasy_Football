Fantasy Football Draft

Welcome to the fantasy football draft! In this game you will create a team with the best players of your choice. Each player is assigned a value based on a range of sources predicting their fantasy values for the 2019 season. You will have one minute per round to select your Quarterback, Runningback, 2 Wide Receivers, Tightend, Kicker, and finally Team Defense. Choose wisely, and good luck! Kick some ass!
